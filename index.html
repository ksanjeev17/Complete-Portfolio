ONE STOP SHOP.

Welcome to One Stop Shop. Marvel! Here's What It's All About.

This website is a guide to everything Marvel Cinematic Universe related where you can find reviews and rankings. There are three main sections of this website: Movies, TV Shows, and Streaming Services. The Movies tab includes a full ranking of the top ten released Marvel movies with brief reviews that support their rankings. The TV Shows tab, similar to Movies, is a ranking with descriptions of all Marvel TV Shows recently released on Disney+. Lastly, the streaming services tab is what Movies and TV Shows are on which services so you can best find out where to watch what you want to watch.

Below, you will find a full overview of each of the different Marvel movies in each phase. There are four different phases, each of which signify a new chapter in the overarching plot.

Phase One:

The beginning of the entire universe. This was the introduction to Marvel to experienced and new audeince members that gave deep backgrounds to all of the mainstream characters

Phase Two:

"The bridge between our world, and the weirder world" - Nick Fury. Phase Two helped develop the overall storylines, expand the universe to include extraterrestrial life, and establish the advanced technology surrounding these heros

Phase Three:

This is where Marvel got creative with their heros and their storylines. Now finally having an established reputation alongside significant funding, more resources were invested into the CGI of the films to create even more magical and otherwordly experiences.

Phase Four:

Marvel introduced TV Shows here to expand the character development as well as fill in gaps on historical and unfinished plotlines. This ongoing part of the universe is expected to start a whole new storyline and create a framework for the new age of Marvel.
